⚕️ CME’s Personal Duty under Cap. 633

Chief Medical Executives hold specific legal responsibility for day-to-day clinic administration, including patient data security and privacy.

During DH on-site inspections, you and your clinical team must be able to clearly demonstrate how sensitive Patient Personal Data is protected.

Source: Office for Regulation of Private Healthcare Facilities (ORPHF)
